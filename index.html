LiveLock™

Interactive Smart Door Experience

The Problem

Many people experience physical limitations that make standard doors difficult to open and close. Individuals with arthritis, carpal tunnel, limb differences, and mobility aids often face barriers created by traditional door hardware and locking systems.

Our Solution

LiveLock™ is a smart interior door system designed for inclusive access. It uses voice commands, facial recognition, fingerprint scanning, and palm pattern recognition to create hands-free access. The smart panel and mobile app allow full customization, alerts, and remote control.

Materials

  • Solid core composite door
  • Recycled aluminum inner frame
  • Tempered smart glass touch panel
  • Integrated biometric sensor modules
